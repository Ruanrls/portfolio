# Embaré

Embaré is a Dairy and Candies industry. The company is known for its
brands and products such as milk and caramel.

## Challenge

During my journey at the company, I maintained some internal systems
and I also had the opportunity to participate in the emergence of two new projects,
among them an e-commerce system aimed at selling products
company directly to its customers. A chatbot was also developed so that
customers could ask questions about the products and the company, follow up on their orders and
request invoices.
