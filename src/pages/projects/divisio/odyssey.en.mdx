# About

Odyssey is a platform that connects students with companies in
job market. With the possibility of candidacies and creation
of online events via streaming.

## Challenge

The biggest challenge of this project was dealing with the complexity of
schedules. For events possessed a vast amount of
rules in addition to having the possibility of being recurrent.
